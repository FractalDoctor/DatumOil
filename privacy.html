DATUMOIL — PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 7 March 2026

DATA COLLECTION

DatumOil does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry.

NETWORK REQUESTS

The app makes network requests to the following services to retrieve publicly available data:

• Yahoo Finance — for crude oil prices and market indicator data

• Google News RSS — for oil and energy news headlines

No user-identifying information is included in these requests.

APPLE INTELLIGENCE

Sentiment analysis is performed entirely on-device using Apple's FoundationModels framework. No headline text or analysis results are transmitted to any server. All processing occurs locally on your device.

LOCAL STORAGE

The app caches price data, market indicators, and scored headlines locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is used solely for offline display and is not shared with any third party.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

DatumOil does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. There are zero third-party SDKs.

CHILDREN

DatumOil is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

CHANGES

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

CONTACT

For questions about this privacy policy or the app, please open an issue on GitHub.
