DATUMOIL — SUPPORT

OVERVIEW

DatumOil is a retro-styled crude oil price monitor with live market indicators and AI-powered news sentiment analysis. All data is fetched directly — no accounts, no API keys, no servers.

DATA SOURCES

  • Brent and WTI crude futures prices — Yahoo Finance
  • Market indicators (OVX, Natural Gas, Dollar Index) — Yahoo Finance
  • News headlines — Google News RSS (oil/energy)
  • Sentiment scoring — Apple Intelligence (on-device)

PRICE TAB

Displays live Brent and WTI futures prices with 7-day, 30-day, and 90-day chart views. Includes optional 5-day and 20-day moving averages for both benchmarks, plus the Brent-WTI spread with contango/backwardation labelling. Prices refresh automatically every 15 minutes.

VIBE INDEX

The fuel gauge on the Price tab shows the Vibe Index — a rolling sentiment score calculated from recent oil news headlines. The needle sweeps from bearish (left) to bullish (right). The first analysis after installing may take a minute or two. Subsequent refreshes are faster as only new headlines are scored.

MARKET TAB

Four key market indicators updated daily: OVX oil volatility index with threat level gauge, Henry Hub natural gas futures, Brent-WTI spread, and US Dollar Index. Each includes a block sparkline and a range bar showing where the current value sits within its 3-month trading range.

APPLE INTELLIGENCE

The Vibe Sentiment feature requires Apple Intelligence to be enabled on your device (Settings > Apple Intelligence & Siri). Each headline is scored by the on-device foundation model with oil market domain awareness. Without Apple Intelligence, cached sentiment data is still displayed if available.

TROUBLESHOOTING

  • No data displayed: Check your internet connection. Price and market data require connectivity on first launch. Pull down to refresh any tab.
  • Sentiment unavailable: Ensure Apple Intelligence is enabled in Settings > Apple Intelligence & Siri.
  • Stale prices: Prices update automatically every 15 minutes when the app is open. Markets are closed on weekends and holidays.
  • Analysis taking a long time: The first sentiment analysis scores each headline individually on-device. This is normal. After that, only new headlines are analysed.
