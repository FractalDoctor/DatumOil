DATUMOIL

Crude Oil Market Monitor

Real-time Brent and WTI crude oil prices, key market indicators, and AI-powered vibe sentiment analysis — all in a retro CRT phosphor aesthetic.

FEATURES

  • Brent & WTI spot prices from the US Energy Information Administration
  • Price charts with 7D / 30D / 90D windows and moving average overlays
  • Crude inventories, refinery utilisation, and US rig count
  • Vibe sentiment analysis powered by Apple Intelligence
  • On-device AI scoring of oil market headlines
  • No tracking, no analytics, no data collection

REQUIRES

iPhone iOS 26+ Apple Intelligence
