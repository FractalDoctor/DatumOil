DATUMOIL — SUPPORT

OVERVIEW

DatumOil displays crude oil market data from the U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA) and analyses news headlines using Apple Intelligence.

DATA SOURCES

  • Brent and WTI spot prices — EIA Open Data API (daily)
  • Crude inventories — EIA Weekly Petroleum Status Report
  • Refinery utilisation — EIA Weekly Petroleum Status Report
  • US rig count — EIA Drilling Summary
  • News headlines — Google News RSS
  • Sentiment scoring — Apple Intelligence (FoundationModels, on-device)

APPLE INTELLIGENCE

The Vibe Sentiment feature requires Apple Intelligence to be enabled on your device. Each headline is scored by the on-device foundation model with oil market domain awareness. If Apple Intelligence is unavailable, the sentiment tab will indicate this.

PRICE DATA

Prices are updated daily when markets are open. Data is cached locally for offline viewing. The barrel graphic fill level reflects the current Brent price.

MARKET DATA

Market indicators (inventories, refinery utilisation, rig count) are updated weekly. Tap any indicator panel to see an 8-week sparkline trend.

TROUBLESHOOTING

  • No data displayed: Check your internet connection. Data is fetched from the EIA API and requires connectivity on first launch.
  • Sentiment unavailable: Ensure Apple Intelligence is enabled in Settings > Apple Intelligence & Siri.
  • Stale prices: Price data updates on app foreground if older than 1 hour. Markets are closed on weekends.
